About

Hello, I'm Heather.

Eighteen years in psychedelic integration. Sixteen in therapeutic bodywork. A practice built from both.

I'm a trauma-informed coach and psychedelic integration specialist. My work supports people through trauma, addiction, and the kind of life transitions that ask everything of you — and helps them turn those experiences into change that actually lasts.

This path isn't only professional. I've moved through my own seasons of trauma, loss, and deep transformation. The years of healing work that asked me to face myself fully — and rebuild from honest ground — are what let me sit with others now, with both depth and discernment.

My approach is grounded, direct, and warm. Real change doesn't come from avoiding what's hard. It comes from learning to move through it, supported and aware, until the difficult thing has been metabolized rather than managed.
