The Approach

Nervous system first. Always.

The insights from a retreat, a medicine journey, or a long season of inner work don't land through force. They land through safety — the kind your body recognizes before your mind does.

My work draws from somatic therapy, polyvagal theory, and contemplative practice. We move slowly. We track what your body is telling us. We let what's wanting to emerge actually have room to emerge.

Who this work is for

If any of this sounds familiar.

After a plant medicine or psychedelic experience

You opened something — and now you're looking for steady, informed support to weave what you touched into your actual life.

Living with trauma or chronic overwhelm

Your nervous system has been carrying a lot for a long time. You're ready to work with a practitioner who won't rush the process.

At a threshold you can't name yet

Something is shifting — grief, a relationship, a calling, a question. You want company for the in-between.
