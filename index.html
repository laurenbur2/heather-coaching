The Approach

Slow, honest, and yours.

Integration isn't about adding more to your life. It's about listening — to what's already present, and what's asking to be tended.

I work with people who are navigating a threshold: after a retreat, a loss, a shift they can't name yet. We move at the pace of your nervous system. Nothing is forced, and nothing is rushed.
