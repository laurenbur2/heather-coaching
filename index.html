The approach

Initiation. Integration. Embodiment.

Initiation — facing what's been avoided, suppressed, or unseen. These moments often arrive uninvited: a crisis, a transition, a call from somewhere underneath the daily mind. I help you move through them with steadiness instead of panic.

Integration — insight alone doesn't change a life. Integration is the slow work of translating what you've touched into how you actually live: your relationships, your patterns, your choices.

Embodiment — real change happens in the body before the mind catches up. Through somatic practice and nervous-system work, we anchor inner shifts into daily life.

This isn't quick-fix work. It's a guided process of change that holds.
