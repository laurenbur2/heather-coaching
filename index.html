The approach

Initiation. Integration. Embodiment.

Initiation — facing what's been avoided, suppressed, or unseen. These moments often arrive uninvited: a crisis, a transition, a call from somewhere underneath the daily mind. I help you move through them with steadiness instead of panic.

Integration — insight alone doesn't change a life. Integration is the slow work of translating what you've touched into how you actually live: your relationships, your patterns, your choices.

Embodiment — real change happens in the body before the mind catches up. Through somatic practice and nervous-system work, we anchor inner shifts into daily life.

This isn't quick-fix work. It's a guided process of change that holds.

Is this for you?

You've done some work. You know there's more.

This work is for people who sense something deeper is asking to be faced. You may be:

Returning from a psychedelic experience

You opened something — a journey, a retreat, a medicine experience — and now you're looking for steady support to weave it into your actual life.

Carrying trauma or chronic overwhelm

Unresolved trauma, abuse, or grief that hasn't softened with time. Anxiety, depression, or a low hum of disconnection you can't shake.

Caught in patterns you can't outrun

Addiction, self-sabotage, attachment wounds, intrusive thoughts — cycles that have outlasted every promise you've made to yourself.

At a threshold you can't yet name

A loss, a relationship ending, an identity shift, a spiritual opening that feels more disorienting than freeing.

You're not looking for surface-level change. You're ready to live differently — and to be honestly supported while you do.
