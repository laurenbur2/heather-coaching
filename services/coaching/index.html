Coaching

Transformational Coaching.

A deep, sustained 1:1 practice for those ready for real personal evolution.

What this is

Shaped by your nervous system. Paced by what's true.

Transformational coaching is the slow, honest work of becoming who this season of your life is asking you to become. We move through shadow, attachment, grief, and the parts of you that have been waiting to be met — and we do it at a pace your body can actually integrate, not the pace ambition wants.

This is not advice-giving or strategy. It is compassionate inquiry, embodied insight, and the kind of accompaniment that holds you steady while real change takes root.

What we work with

The terrain of this practice

Shadow work

Meeting the disowned, denied, or hidden parts of yourself with steadiness rather than shame — and letting what's been in the dark begin to inform how you live.

Attachment awareness

Tracing the patterns of how you bond, protect, withdraw, and reach — and gently reshaping the relational templates that no longer fit who you are.

Grief integration

Tending to losses old and new — the visible ones and the ones that never had a name — so they can move through you instead of staying lodged.

Inner-child healing

Returning to the younger places in you that are still carrying the weight, and offering the kind of attention they didn't get the first time.

Compassionate inquiry

Asking the questions that get underneath the story — without judgment, without rush — until the body and the truth meet in the same room.

The long arc

Not a quick reframe or a single insight. The sustained practice of letting your life slowly reorganize around what you've actually come to know.

How sessions unfold

The shape of the work

We begin with inquiry

A complimentary discovery call to feel out the fit, hear what's bringing you here, and shape an initial direction together. Nothing committed until both of us know this is the right work.

Sessions are 60 to 90 minutes

Held weekly or biweekly, online via video, with occasional in-person availability in Austin, TX. Sessions move between dialogue, somatic attention, and quiet — paced to what the day asks for.

Containers are arc-shaped

Most clients work with me in defined arcs — three months, six months, or longer — rather than session-by-session. The form gives the work room to deepen and the patterns time to actually shift.

Pricing is shared in conversation

Because each engagement is shaped to fit, pricing is discussed once we know what we're building. A limited number of reduced-fee spots are available each quarter.
