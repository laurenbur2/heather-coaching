Common questions

Before we begin

Is this therapy?

No. Coaching and integration work are distinct from psychotherapy. If you're actively in crisis or need clinical mental health care, I'll help you find the right support.

Do you provide psychedelic substances or facilitate journeys?

No. I work only with preparation and integration — the time before and after a journey. I can offer referrals to reputable, legal facilitators if helpful.

How does pricing work?

Each offering is shaped to the individual, so pricing is shared in our initial conversation once we've discussed fit, scope, and what you're working with. A limited number of reduced-fee spots are available each quarter.

Where are sessions held?

Online via video, with occasional in-person availability in Austin, TX. Session length varies by offering.

What does a discovery call look like?

A complimentary 20-minute conversation. We'll talk briefly about what's bringing you here, what you're hoping for, and whether this work is the right fit. No pressure either way.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Life happens. Please give at least 24 hours notice when possible. Late cancellations are considered case by case.
