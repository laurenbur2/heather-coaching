Common questions

Before we begin

Is this therapy?

No. Coaching is distinct from psychotherapy. If you're actively in crisis or need clinical mental health care, I'll help you find the right support.

Do you provide psychedelic substances or facilitate journeys?

No. I work only with integration — the time before and after a journey. I can offer referrals to reputable, legal facilitators if helpful.

Where are sessions held?

Online via video, with occasional in-person availability in [City]. Sessions are 60 minutes unless otherwise arranged.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Life happens. Please give at least 24 hours notice when possible. Late cancellations are considered case by case.

Do you offer sliding scale?

Yes — a limited number of reduced-fee spots are offered each quarter. Reach out to inquire.
